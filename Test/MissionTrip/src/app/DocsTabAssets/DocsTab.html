Uploaded Documents

Please make a scan or take a photo on your phone of the photo page of your passport and upload it here.

To do this selecet the icon to the side of the input field. This will open up a folder browser and from here you will need to navigate to
the folder that your document is located in. Select it and hit okay. Provide a document description for the document you have just
uploaded then click save. The document will be loaded into the system and then appear on the table below
