Emergency Contacts

Please add details for two people we can contact in an emergency. This could be parents, children, siblings as appropriate. To do this click the
'Add Emergency Contact' button and input the details of your contact into the pop up window. They will then be displayed in the tabel below.
To edit any details of a contact, select the person on the list, then click the edit button When you are done, click on the ‘Health’ tab to continue.
